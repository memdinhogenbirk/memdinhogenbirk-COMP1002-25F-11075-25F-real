CONSOLE TECHNICAL SPECS

MEMORY

  • Main Work RAM,64 KB (16-bit),General-purpose for 68000 CPU
  • Video RAM (VRAM),64 KB,"Graphics data (tiles, sprites)"
  • Audio RAM,8 KB,For Z80 sound operations
  • Cartridge ROM,Up to 4 MB (32 Mbit),Game data
  • VSRAM,512 bits (20 words x 11-bit),Horizontal/vertical scrolling
  • CRAM,"128 bytes (32 words x 15-bit, 4 palettes x 16 colors)",Color palettes

VIDEO

  • Resolutions,"Progressive: 320x224 or 256x224 (NTSC); 320x240 or 256x240 (PAL)
  • Interlaced: 320x448/256x448 (NTSC); 320x480/256x480 (PAL)"
  • Colors,"512-color palette; 61 on-screen (4 palettes of 16, incl. transparent)
  • Supports shadow/highlight modes"
  • Sprites,"80 on-screen (20 per scanline max)
  • Up to 32x32 pixels (4x4 tiles); 16 colors each"
  • Background Planes,"2 scrollable planes (A: foreground, B: background)
  • Tile-based (32x32 px tiles); sizes up to 1024x1024
  • Window plane (non-scrolling subset of Plane A)"
  • DMA,CPU-to-VDP transfers during H/V-Blank
  • Clock,~13 MHz

AUDIO

  • Yamaha YM2612,"6-channel FM synthesis + 1 PCM channel (8/9-bit samples)
  • Operator-based waveforms; ~53 kHz sample rate"
  • Texas Instruments SN76489 (PSG),3 tone + 1 noise channel; integrated in VDP
  • Output,Stereo (via headphone jack on Model 1)
