90 Day Limited Warranty

Cartridges and Consoles are covered by the 90 DAY LIMITED WARRANTY

If a defect occurs during the 90 day time-frame, SEGA will repair or replace it.

If a defect occurs outside of the 90 day time-frame, you may contact the Sega Consumer Service.

Defects caused by negligence, accidents, unreasonable use, or tampering are not covered by the warranty.
Additionally, if the serial number has been removed, altered, or defaced in anyway, the warranty is void.

Please retain your sales receipt to easily establish the date of purchase for in-warranty repairs

For all repair or replacement inquiries, please call the Sega Service Department at 1-800-USA-SEGA.

Console Trouble Shooting

No picture on screen.

  1. Is the Cartridge pushed FIRMLY into the Game Slot?
  2. Is the Genesis Console turned on?
  3. Is the AC Adaptor plugged in properly?
  4. Is the TV turned on?
  5. Is there power to the wall socket?
  6. Is the channel switch setting on the back of the Genesis Console the same as the TV channel being used?
  7. Have you confused the RF Jack with the Video or Power-In Adaptor Jacks?
  8. Review the connections between the Antenna Cable, Genesis Console, and TV terminals.

Screen flickers or no color.

  1. Are the Antenna Cables connected properly?
  2. Is the TV channel fine adjustment set properly?
  3. Are the TV color controls set properly?
  4. Is the Genesis Cartridge inserted properly?

No game sound.

  1. Is the TV volume too low, or off?
  2. Have the connection cables come loose, or gotten disconnected?

Picture on TV screen is unsteady.

  1. Adjust the vertical and horizontal hold controls on the TV.

Fringes appear on TV during a game.

  1. Adjust the manual fine tuning adjustment controls on the TV.

Fringes appear on TV during a broadcast.

  1. Is the Antenna Cable connected properly?
  2. Make sure that you have turned off the Power Switch on the Genesis Console.

No Power Light.

  1. Is there power to the wall socket?

If you continue to have a problem, use this manual to carefully re-connect the system, step by step.
If the problem still exists, please call the Sega Consumer Service Department, toll free, at 1-800-USA-SEGA.
